Alumna Leaves Nearly $1 Million to MHSA

Mary Santarsiero, a 1970 graduate of the MHSA program (then known as Hospital and Health Services Administration), died in 2011 at the age of 89 after truly living a life of service. She wasn't quite finished. With her last wish, part of her estate went to the MHSA program at Xavier. In all, her gift to the program totaled more than $900,000.
