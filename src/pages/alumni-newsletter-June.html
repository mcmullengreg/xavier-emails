This spring, fans were able to watch Xavier defeat Maryland in the NCAA Tournament, then spend the next day following Jason Kokrak around Bay Hill golf course in the same town. It was a "perfect week" in Orlando.

Xavier Baseball

The Musketeers won their third BIG EAST title in four years on the same weekend Jim Bunning passed away. It was appropriate the team could honor a legend and make the NCAA Tournament for the second consecutive year.
