Below is a list of templates and template parts put together for various email communications executed by Xavier University.

Every "page" requires a YAML header and includes the subject and email description. The subject is the "title"; the description is the hidden preheader for Email clients with a preview section.

The preheader should be no more than 140 characters but no less than 45. Preheader should also be customized either for the entire email campaign, OR for individual emails. This will likely not be able to be edited via Slate so use as needed.
