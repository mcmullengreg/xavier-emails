New Fitness Center to Be Built Near Cintas

The 20-year relationship between TriHealth and Xavier has reached new heights, officials announced in January. In the continuing partnership to educate and heal our communities, the pair are developing a new fitness center, to be located at Cleneay Avenue and Musketeer Drive near the residence halls and the Cintas Center.
