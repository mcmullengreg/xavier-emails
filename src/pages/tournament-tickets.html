Men's NCAA Tournament Information

Xavier has earned a No. 11 Seed in the 2017 NCAA Tournament and will face No. 6 seeded Maryland (24-8) in Orlando, Fla. on Thursday, March 16 at 6:50 p.m. at Amway Center.

Group Ticket Information

$66 per ticket, upper level
Limit 2 per person.

Tickets are for Thursday's evening session only in Xavier's group block. Should Xavier advance, you are also required to buy the same number of tickets for Saturday's session in Orlando. Those tickets will be the same price. However, if Xavier loses on Thursday, you will not be charged and will not receive any future tickets.

Purchase by calling the Xavier Athletics Ticket Office at 513-745-3411 x7 beginning at 9 a.m. today, Tuesday, March 14.
