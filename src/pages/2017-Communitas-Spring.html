As an alumnus or friend of Xavier, you are receiving Communitas, the magazine of the College of Professional Sciences.

This issue explores Xavier's new strategic partnership with TriHealth, which will result in construction of the Health United Building—The HUB—where a new rec center, a new health and wellness center, and several health-related academic programs will be housed by the fall of 2019. Plus you'll find stories about a new undergraduate nursing program for people who want to change careers, and the Montessori Dementia Care program that provides training for retirement communities.

Read Communitas online. We welcome your feedback. Please send comments to professionalsciences@xavier.edu.
