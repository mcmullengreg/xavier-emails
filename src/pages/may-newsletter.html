Xavier Stories Will Come Your Way Via New Channels

We know you like a good story about Xavier. And we here at Xavier love to tell them.

 {{> spacer size=32 }} {{> spacer size=32 }}

Like many other universities as well as other entities across the country, we at Xavier are committing to utilize a variety of communication channels to disseminate great Xavier stories. Gone are the days of having to wait for a Magazine, where everything is read in one place, at one time.

Today, with the popularity of social media, video storytelling and our website, we have learned just how you're reading our content — and because of that knowledge, we are delivering stories to you in an easier and more timely way.

That said, who doesn't like to get some Xavier stories in the mail too? Stay tuned for some exciting and new items in your mailbox that will allow you to hear even more about what's happening at X. One should be coming soon.

Make sure you are ready for stories on all Xavier channels:

    • Do you get the Alumni E-Newsletter? Make sure we have your current contact info.

    • Do you follow us on social media?

    • Have you heard about our new Xavier Storytellers web page?

    • Did you know all of your Class Notes and Alumni events can be found online?

Get more of the Xavier stories you love. Everyday. Lets' Go X!
